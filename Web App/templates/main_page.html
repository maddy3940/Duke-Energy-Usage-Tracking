Duke Energy Usage Tracking

Enter number of hours worked in HH.HH/HH or HH MM format

Example- 1) 19.20/20.00 - This means you have worked 19.2 hours out of 20 and the result in this case will be 0.8 hours

Example- 2) 19 12/20.00 - This means you have worked 19 hours 12 mins out of 20 and the result in this case will be 0.8 hours

The length of the input should be 11 characters including slash and space or dot
